Course Module

Module I -ISO 9001:2015/14001/18001/17025/OPEX/COMEX STANDARD Management Briefing (1 day)

This module gives an overview of the fundamental principles and requirements of the ISO 9001 series standards it explains and clarifies management responsibilities as well as other requirements of the clauses of the standards, while elaborating the similarities and the differences between the contractual standards (ISO 9001). This module also presents the route to quality system certification to ISO 9000 contractual standards. Module is recommended for Top Management Staff of Organisations.

Module II -Internal Quality System Audit (2 days)

This module treats the clauses of the ISO 9001 and what is necessary to be incorporated into the Organisationâ€™s business structure for a good Quality System and addresses the principles and the practice of quality system audit focusing more attention on internal quality audit functions. Recommended for training on Internal Auditors of quality systems

Module III -Quality Systems Documentation (3weeks)

This module deals with the various documentation necessary for establishing sound quality system for the purpose of Quality System Certification. It deals with the preparation of the Quality Manual, its maintenance and the principles of document control. Recommended for all preparing for certification of their quality system, especially those in the implementing team.

Module IV -Guide to preparation of the Quality Manual (1 day)

This module is a briefing of the requirement of the ISO Guide 10013 â€“ Quality Manual-Guideline to preparation and explains in detail the content of the Quality Manual. Recommended for those preparing the QM, auditors and operators of the quality system in accordance to ISO 9000 series standards.

Module V -Risked-Based thinking Analysis Tools (6 day)

Statistics have shown that 90% of employees are reactive instead of proactive in their approach to work. This often leads to stress, wastage of resources and time, and eventually loss of revenue. With this training, they will be equipped with tools that allow them to identify potential challenges/risks in their processes even before they occur, enabling them to mitigate or avoid them. The training spans four weeks and includes practical sessions specially designed to have minimal impact on your organization's daily operations because we value your valuable time.
